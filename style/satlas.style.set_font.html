satlas.style.set_font¶

satlas.style.set_font(font='Palatino Linotype', family='serif')[source]¶

Sets the font to the chosen family and the family options to the supplied fonts:

Parameters:
  • font (str or list) – Name or list of names of fonts.
  • family (str) – Family name of the fonts that will be set and used.
